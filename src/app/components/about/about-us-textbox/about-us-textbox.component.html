“All of the goals are aimed for everyone. So, every single individual can make a change for the world. But the biggest change can happen through the corporate world. “

This website was developed by 4 Danish students at Academy of Technologies and Business Zealand. /FutureWorld tries to provide answers to some of the most critical questions for companies and try to give inspiration for how work with the goals in mind. So regardless of whether you as a top manager need to understand sustainability as a new basic condition, or whether you s a middle manager need to find inspiration from others or insight into methods that can be useful in your work, there is help here.
